Hotels

  1. The Avery
    2616 P St NW, Washington, DC 20007
    4-Star hotel with a bar/lounge. Near Georgetown University. Includes WIFI and breakfast.
  2. Homewood Suites by Hilton
    50 M St SE, Washington, DC 20003
    3-Star Hotel with an indoor pool. Under 10 minute walk to the Capitol Riverfront and two miles from the City Center. Includes WIFI, fitness center, breakfast buffet
    The visitors center offers guided walking tours and trolley tours. Trolley tours are $12/adult $9/senior $6/child. You are also welcome to self-tour at your leisure.
    Photographs are welcome, but please refrain from capturing funerals and loved ones visiting grave sites.
  3. Washington Plaza
    10 Thomas Cir NW, Washington, DC 20005
    3.5-Star hotel with an outdoor pool. 0.6 miles from the WHite House. On-site restaurant/lounge. Includes WIFI, fitness center, breakfast
  4. Hotel 1600
    1600 Rhode Island Ave NW, Washington, DC 20036
    3-Star hotel near the White House. Includes WIFI, fitness center, breakfast
  5. Holiday Inn
    550 C St SW, Washington, DC 20024
    3-Star hotel with an on-site restaurant and Starbucks. Top-rated location. 11 minute walk to the US Capitol, 20 minute walk to the Washington Monument, 30 minute walk to the White House. Includes WIFI, an outdoor pool, fitness center
  6. The Hamilton Hotel
    1001 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20005
    4-Star hotel located in the Downtown City Center. On-site restaurant, bar, Starbucks. Less than 0.5 miles from the Washington Monument. Includes WIFI, fitness center
  7. Washington Court Hotel
    525 New Jersey Ave NW, Washington, DC 20001
    4-Star hotel with restaurant located in the Capitol Hill neighborhood. 1.4 miles from the City Center, 10 minute walk to the US Capitol. Includes WIFI, fitness center
  8. Hilton Garden Inn
    815 14th St NW, Washington, DC 20005
    3-Star hotel in a prime location. Two blocks from the White House, 12 minute walk to the Washington Monument. On-site restaurant/bar. Includes WIFI, indoor pool, fitness center
  9. Quality Inn and Suites
    1600 New York Ave NE, Washington, DC 20002
    2.5-Star hotel in Washington's Brentwood neighborhood. Just under three miles to the Supreme Court and Library of Congress. Includes WIFI, breakfast, fitness center
  10. Americana Hotel
    1400 Richmond Hwy, Arlington, VA 22202
    2.5-Star budget-friendly hotel near Arlington National Cemetery. Includes WIFI, breakfast, airport shuttle
